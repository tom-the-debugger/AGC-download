AGC-Client


Controls

  • v: Reset health/reset death timer
  • b: Frozen bullets
  • i: Randomize name and ID
  • z: Grappling hook. Will only work with Long Arms enabled
  • x: Teleport to the target's mouse pointer
  • o: Show mod menu
  • h: Teleport to target
  • j: Randomly find target
  • k: Chat
  • l: Target player
  • p: Load player values
  • 1-9: Gun switcher (disabled on mode 0)
  • `: Become headless and only a wheel
  • ~: Comes back from headlessness
  • Chat > ?: Typing "?" in chat will clone yourself as it is not a valid character. With mode 4 it will do 30 clones.

Mod Description

  • Godmode: Makes you invincible
  • Revive: Resets the death timer and restores you to full health
  • Crazy Clones: Spawns 32 clones around the target all pointing towards the target
  • Teleport: Teleports to target
  • No-Clip/Flight: Phases through walls and lets you fly
  • Unsafe Zone: Makes the safe zone unsafe
  • All Perks: Gives all the perks to you
  • Long Arms: You get extra reach
  • Infinite Ammo: Infinite shots
  • No KB: You don't take any knockback
  • Auto Revive: Automatically revives you where you die
  • Aimbot: Automatically aims towards the target
  • Bhop: Starts moving around the target automatically
  • Show Bots: Shows bots
  • Hide Players: Hides all players except you
  • Auto Shoot: Automatically shoots for you. Useful with bots
  • Black Robot: Sets your color to Infinity so you become a black robot
  • Mode 0: Regular player
  • Mode 1: Starts making another person look like they are hacking (they cannot see that though). Useful for making a closet cheater blatant
  • Mode 2: "Lags" the player (they cannot see that though)/mirror their skin and ID. To mirror their skin and ID but don't lag them set the chat to anything BUT blank and their username
  • Mode 3: Same as "Crazy Clones"
  • Mode 4: Gives a black HP bar (it will look red to you) and color Infinity and sets your default username to "RD-AGC<. Detective style./li>
  • Mode 5: Username switcher/clone the target
  • Auto Detect: Auto detects hackers
  • All Hack: Makes EVERYONE start teleporting (press u to disperse the clones if you are cloning)
  • Flashing HP: On mode 4 it will flash your HP bar, anything else it will turn your HP bar orange
  • Noise: On mode 4 it will give free kills to everyone playing. On mode 0 it will give free kills to the target only.
  • Self Target: It will target yourself but it will not change the actual "Target username" variable.
  • Invis: Makes you invisible (100% foolproof, nobody can see you)
  • No Credit: If someone kills you, they won't get the kill credit
  • Target HUD: Shows the target's visual stuff in the top-right corner (WIP)
  • Spectate: Will turn you invisible and spectate your target
  • Chat/IGN: Same as pressing k key. It will modify the nuke name, the target name (on mode 5) or your own name
  • Target Player: Same as pressing L key. It will prompt you to enter a target's username
  • Add hacker: Adds a user to the hacker list (add a "?" to the beginning of your answer to remove a player, "//" to delete all)
  • Add ID: Adds a user's ID to the ID list (add a "?" to the beginning of your answer to remove an ID, "//" to delete all)
  • Whitelist: Adds someone to the whitelist (add a "?" to the beginning of your answer to remove a player, "//" to delete all)
  • Block Player: Blocks a player so you can't see them
  • Speed modifier: Multiplies your speed by that amount
  • Jump modifier: Multiplies your jump by that amount
  • Gravity modifier: Multiplies your gravity by that number
  • Clone modifier: Creates more clones. Higher number means more clones but more lag
  • Show/Hide Vars: Shows/hides the variables
