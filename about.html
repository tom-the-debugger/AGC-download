AGC-Client


Controls

  • v: Reset health/reset death timer
  • b: Frozen bullets
  • i: Randomize name and ID
  • z: Grappling hook. Will only work with Long Arms enabled
  • x: Teleport to the target's mouse pointer
  • o: Show mod menu
  • h: Teleport to target
  • j: Randomly find target
  • k: Chat
  • l: Target player
  • p: Load player values
  • 1-9: Gun switcher (disabled on mode 0)
  • `: Become headless and only a wheel
  • ~: Comes back from headlessness

Mod Description

  • Godmode: Makes you invincible
  • Revive: Resets the death timer and restores you to full health
  • Crazy Clones: Spawns 32 clones around the target all pointing towards the target
  • Teleport: Teleports to target
  • No-Clip/Flight: Phases through walls and lets you fly
  • Unsafe Zone: Makes the safe zone unsafe
  • All Perks: Gives all the perks to you
  • Long Arms: You get extra reach
  • Infinite Ammo: Infinite shots
  • No KB: You don't take any knockback
  • Show Bots: Shows bots
  • Hide Players: Hides all players except you
  • Auto Shoot: Automatically shoots for you. Useful with bots
